Research Experience

Neural Network for Segmentation of Medical Images

Masters' Thesis Work

Developed and trained a deep neural network for the segmentation of medical images during masters' degree research. Also collaborated with other graduate students to explore Active Contour Models integration in neural networks and their application in medical images processing.

Skin Cancer Detection with Deep Neural Networks and Vision Transformers

Book chapter work

Used transfer learning for finetuning different convolutional deep neural networks and vision transformers for classification of cancerous skin images.

Solving PDEs Using Physics Informed Neural Networks (PINNs)

Co-supervised

Worked on developing and training a physics-informed neural network for solving PDEs with a graduate student at GIK Institute as his co-supervisor. Enhanced PINN Performance Through Lie Symmetry Group, and solved Newell-Whitehead-Segel, Allen-Cahn, and Burgers' Equations.

Careem Captain Fraud Detection

Co-Principal Investigator (Co-PI)

Explored different data processing techniques and machine learning algorithms to automate Careem's Captain (driver) fraud detection. Careem is a well-known ride-hailing services provider company in the Middle East region.

Neural Network for Detection of Hidden Explosives and Narcotics

Journal Research Paper

Developed a deep neural network-based approach for the rapid detection of hidden explosives and narcotics using Multipulse Nuclear Quadrupole Resonance (NQR).

AI-based Service Request Analysis and Rate Limiting

Principal Investigator (PI)

Leading this project from the National Database and Registration Authority (NADRA) to automate anomaly detection followed by rate limiting of requests for different services in NADRA.
